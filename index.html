our work

Cosulting

Protecting Lands And Resources

Providing Spaces For Sharing Between First Nations

We serve 43 First Nations communities in Quebec and Labrador

We are guided by Etuaptmumk, which means Two-Eyed Seeing. This principle combines Indigenous and scientific knowledge to turn First Nations’ visions for sustainable development into reality. Our joint achievements are made possible thanks to our expertise and the bonds and strong relationships that we build with First Nations and our partners.

For over 20 years, our multidisciplinary team has worked with First Nations to implement their vision for sustainable development. Our work is guided by the needs, priorities and issues identified by First Nations.
